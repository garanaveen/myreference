How to clear cookies from specific site on google chrome browser?
Settings->Privacy & Security->SiteSettings->Cookies and Site Data -> See all cookies and site data.
Delete "account.google.com" entries
If thats not good enough deleting box.com related cookies should be good enough or google.com related.
Keywords : clear, Chrome, box.com, cookies, cache,

Another easy way to go to the right screen is : Search:cookies and then select "Third party cookies" and then select "See all site data and permissions"
