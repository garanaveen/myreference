How to work with workspaces in Ubuntu?
--------------------------------------
Workspaces have been made vertical in latest version of ubuntu.
Switching to workspace below -> <Ctrl>+<Alt>+DownArrow or WindowsKey+PageDown (#Desktop, #Ubuntu, #Workspace, #Move)
Switching to workspace above -> <Ctrl>+<Alt>+UpArrow or WindowsKey+PageUp
https://help.ubuntu.com/stable/ubuntu-help/shell-workspaces-switch.html.en


Workspaces in Mate,
Right click workspaces and go to preferences and make the workspaces vertical (with preferably just 2 workspaces).
Use <Ctrl>+<Alt>+Up/DownArrow to switch between workspaces.  (Deprecated?)
