Plantronics 5200 UC comes with a BT600 Adapter for connectivity.

Following is the mapping for Sound menu on ubuntu,
Analog Output - BT600 : Plantronics 5200 - This analog option seems to be relatively better with connectivity.
Digital Output - BT600 : Plantronics 5200 - This was having PC disconnected issues.


Play sound test from terminal (plays for 4 seconds).
speaker-test  --nperiods 10 --nloops 1

Related commands : aplay
